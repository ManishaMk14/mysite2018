A cat's nose is ridged with a unique pattern, just like a human fingerprint. Cats have scent glands along their tail, their forehead, lips, chin, and the underside of their front paws. ... A cat rubs against people to mark its territory.

Adopt

Before you rush into adopting a cat, do realise that having a pet is a big responsibility. Adopting a pet means you need to commit to and take care of it for the rest of its life. They cost time and money and will require medical attention when ill or injured. It is important that the pet is accepted by the entire household too.
